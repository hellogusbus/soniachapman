"You might be feeling overwhelmed, stuck, or disconnected from yourself. I offer a supportive space where you can feel safe, heard, and not judged."

I'm Sonia, a BACP Accredited psychotherapist with over 10 years of experience supporting people through some of life's most challenging times. I work with people who are navigating life transitions, relationship difficulties, burnout, and the impact of trauma, anxiety, or depression.

My approach is client-centred, collaborative, and integrative — we focus on what feels most important to you. Together, we'll work at your pace to explore your experiences, process difficult emotions, and understand what you need.

I draw on a range of therapeutic approaches, including EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitisation and Reprocessing) and Interpersonal Therapy, tailoring our work together to suit your individual needs.

I have particular experience supporting people affected by domestic abuse, grief, loss, and depression, and have worked extensively with young adults, neurodiverse people, those with experience of adoption or being in care, and members of LGBTQIA+ communities.

My practice is based in South Liverpool, in purpose-built therapy rooms nestled among trees and natural surroundings — a calming and restorative space for healing and growth. I am also a BUPA Recognised therapist and currently accepting BUPA patients.
